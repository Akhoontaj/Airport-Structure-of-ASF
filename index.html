    CSO

    CSO means Chief Security Officer

    Responsibilities of CSO

  1. CSO is over all responsible of the airport security of concerned airport
  2. CSO is responsible for the operatonal matters in the concerned airport
  3. CSO is responsible for the administration of ASF of their concerned airport
  4. CSO is responsible for the provision of good food, residence and other living and health facilities
  5. CSO is responsible for their salaries, financial matters as well as official matters

    6. Structure of under command offices/officers

  6. Ops Officer
  7. Admin Officer
  8. Accounts Officer
