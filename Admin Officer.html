    Admin Officer

    Admin Officer is the 2nd hand command of Ops officer as well as of CSO

  1. Admin officer responsible for maintaining of personal files of the officers/officials
  2. Admin officer is responsible for maintaining the record of offences as well as reward of ASF staff
  3. Admin officer is responsible to maintain the record of tours of officers/officials
  4. Admin officer is responsible to maintain the record of posting/transfer/attachment/detachment etc
